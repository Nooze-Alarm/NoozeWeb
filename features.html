Everything You Need to Sleep Better

From intelligent alarms to social challenges, Nooze is packed with features designed to transform your mornings.

Smart Wake Technology

Wake up during your lightest sleep phase, not mid-dream.

Configurable Window

Choose a 15, 20, or 30-minute smart window before your alarm time. Nooze picks the best moment within that range.

Hybrid Detection

Uses your phone's accelerometer and microphone together to accurately detect sleep phases without a wearable.

Sleep Stage Analysis

Identifies light, deep, and REM sleep stages. View detailed breakdowns in your sleep analytics dashboard.

Wake Challenges

No snooze button. Instead, you prove you're awake with real-world challenges that get you out of bed and moving.

NFC Tag Scan

Place an NFC tag in your bathroom or kitchen. Scan it to dismiss the alarm.

Photo Capture

Take a photo to prove you're out of bed. Gets you moving immediately.

Gamification System

Turn waking up into a rewarding daily habit with XP, levels, quests, and achievements.

XP Rewards

10-50 XP per wake-up, 5 XP per journal entry, up to 30 XP streak bonus daily.

Levels & Titles

Progressive leveling system with unlockable titles and achievement badges.

Quests & Achievements

Complete daily/weekly quests and unlock permanent achievements for milestones.

Social Features

Wake up is better with friends. Compete, challenge, and motivate each other.

  • Add friends by username search
  • XP-based leaderboards
  • Wake duels — challenge friends to wake up first
  • Activity feed showing friend progress

Sleep Aids

Built-in calming soundscapes and ambient noise with auto-stop timer to help you fall asleep faster.

Sleep Journal

Smart Home
