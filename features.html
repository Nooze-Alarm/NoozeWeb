Core technology

Smart Wake Window

Pick a 15, 20 or 30 minute window before your alarm. Nooze fires when you're already in light sleep, never mid-REM.

Hybrid Detection

Gradual Volume Ramp

Volume rises softly across the last few minutes so you ease into the morning instead of jolting awake.

Hybrid sleep detection

Tracks Four Sleep Phases

Awake, Light, Deep and REM — detected through your phone's motion and microphone, fused with wearable data when you have one.

  • ▸ Confidence-weighted detection: wearable > on-body sensor fusion > nightstand mic.
  • ▸ Shared-bed mode raises the threshold so a partner's movement isn't logged as yours.
  • ▸ Wearable-only mode skips on-phone sensors entirely if you trust your watch.
  • ▸ Snore and sleep-talk events surfaced in your morning summary.

And Much More

Health Hub

Connects through Health Connect — pulls sleep, heart-rate and SpO₂ data from compatible wearables and apps.

Smart Home

Trigger lights, blinds and devices on wake via Home Assistant. Bring up your bedroom on your schedule.

Sleep Aids

Calming soundscapes — rain, ocean, white noise — to help you drift off. Auto-fade so they don't run all night.

Sleep Journal

Log mood, energy, dreams and notes against each session. Patterns surface over weeks, not screenshots.

Cross-Device Sync

Your alarms, gamification and friends sync across phones. Raw biometrics stay local on each device.

12 Themes

Six free, six premium animated themes. Pick a vibe — from minimal mono to reactive auroras.

Privacy First
