Why Nooze?

Traditional alarms rip you from deep sleep. Nooze works with your body's natural rhythms to make every morning better.

Sleep Cycle Tracking

Monitors your sleep phases through motion and sound analysis. Detects light, deep, and REM sleep to understand your patterns.

Smart Wake Window

Set a 15, 20, or 30-minute window before your alarm. Nooze wakes you during your lightest sleep phase within that range.

No Snooze. Ever.
