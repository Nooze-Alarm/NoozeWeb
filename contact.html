Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Data Collection

Nooze collects minimal data necessary for alarm and sleep tracking functionality. This includes sleep patterns, alarm settings, and basic app usage data. We do not collect data beyond what is needed to provide our core services.

Data Storage

Your data is encrypted and stored primarily on your device. When you enable cloud sync, data is transmitted securely and stored in encrypted form on our servers. You can use Nooze entirely offline if you prefer local-only storage.

Data Sharing

We never sell your personal information to third parties. Data is only shared when necessary to provide the service (e.g., cloud sync) or when required by law. Health data from connected devices stays on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

Your Rights

You can access, modify, or delete your data at any time through the app settings. You can request a complete deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at noozealarm@gmail.com.
