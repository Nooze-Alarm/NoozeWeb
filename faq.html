Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Nooze.

Nooze is a smart alarm clock and sleep tracker for Android. It monitors your sleep cycles using your phone's sensors, wakes you during light sleep for a more refreshed feeling, and gamifies your morning routine with XP, levels, and challenges.

Nooze is currently available for Android. It's built natively for the best performance and integrates with Android's Health Connect platform.

Nooze uses your phone's accelerometer and microphone to detect sleep phases. You set a smart wake window (15, 20, or 30 minutes before your alarm). Nooze monitors your sleep and triggers the alarm when it detects you're in light sleep within that window, resulting in a more natural wake-up.

Yes! All core features are free: unlimited alarms, smart wake, sleep tracking, gamification, social features, 6 themes, and cloud sync. Nooze Premium (€0.99/month or €9.99/year) adds animated themes, premium profile backgrounds, exclusive titles, and advanced analytics.

By design. Sleep science shows that hitting snooze fragments your sleep and actually makes you groggier. Instead, Nooze uses wake challenges (NFC scanning or photo capture) to ensure you're fully awake, and rewards you with XP for getting up.

Yes. Place your phone near your bed and Nooze monitors your sleep using motion and sound analysis. For more detailed data, it also integrates with Health Connect and wearable devices like Fitbit, Garmin, Oura, Whoop, Polar, Withings, and Suunto.

Nooze integrates with 10 health providers: Android Health Connect (native), Fitbit, Garmin, Oura, Whoop, Polar, Withings, Suunto, Huawei, and Samsung Health (via Health Connect). These provide heart rate, SpO2, and respiratory rate data.

You earn XP for waking up on time (10-50 XP), writing journal entries (5 XP), and maintaining streaks (up to 30 XP bonus). XP lets you level up, complete quests, unlock achievements, and earn exclusive titles.

Privacy is a top priority. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We prioritize local storage, never sell your information, and give you full control to delete your data at any time through the app.
