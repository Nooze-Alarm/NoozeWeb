Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Nooze

Nooze is a next-generation alarm clock app for iOS, Android, Windows, and macOS, designed to help you wake up smarter and feel more refreshed. It uses intelligent sleep tracking and smart wake technology to wake you at the optimal time in your sleep cycle.

Nooze is available for iOS and Android mobile devices, with full support for Windows and macOS desktop applications. All platforms sync seamlessly through the cloud, so your alarms are always available across all your devices.

Nooze uses advanced algorithms to monitor your sleep cycle through movement and sound analysis. It detects when you're in light sleep and wakes you during that phase, rather than during deep sleep. This results in a more natural, refreshing wake-up experience. The app learns your sleep patterns over time to improve accuracy.
